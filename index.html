Welcome Home (Flamingo & Maina Birds)

Some places are built to impress.

We built FAM to make people feel at home.

After spending nearly a decade in India's metro cities, our founders, Karanvir Singh Sapra and Rahul Yadav, returned to the mountains with a simple idea—to create the kind of place they would always want to come back to. A place where mornings begin with mountain views instead of alarms, where conversations need countless cups of tea, and where every guest is welcomed like an old friend.

Flamingo aur Maina (FAM) is nestled in the heart of Jibhi, surrounded by an apple orchard, 1.5 acres of open land, and uninterrupted 360° mountain views. Every corner of the property has been thoughtfully designed to slow you down—from our cozy wooden cabins and comfortable rooms to our warm common spaces where we serve comforting food made with care.

But FAM isn't just about where you sleep.

It's about how you feel while you're here.

Whether you're escaping the city, working remotely, travelling with friends, celebrating something special, or simply looking for a quiet weekend, we hope you leave with more than just photographs.

We hope you leave with stories, friendships, and a reason to come back.

Because at FAM, we're not just hosting guests.

We're building a place people can belong to.

Our vision is to create a place where every traveller feels at home, reconnects with nature, builds meaningful memories, and experiences genuine mountain hospitality.

Inspired by our own journey back to the mountains, we believe true luxury is found in peace, warmth, meaningful conversations, and unforgettable moments.

At Flamingo aur Maina, we are not simply offering rooms.
