LIFE AT FAM

More Than A Stay.
A Collection Of Moments.

Every sunrise, every cup of coffee, every conversation around the bonfire becomes part of your story.

Chapter 01

Wake Up Above The Clouds

Begin your day with the soft light of dawn creeping over snow-capped peaks. The crisp mountain air and rolling fog create a sanctuary of stillness, inviting you to pause and simply be.

Chapter 02

Walk Through Nature

Wander through lush apple orchards that surround our handcrafted wooden retreats. The rustle of leaves and the scent of pine form a natural symphony, grounding you in the present moment.

Chapter 03

Every Meal Feels Homemade

Share wholesome meals prepared with local ingredients in our warm dining space. Fresh, comforting food served with mountain hospitality — every bite tastes like home.

Chapter 04

Evenings You'll Never Forget

Gather around the warm glow of a crackling bonfire as the sun sets behind the mountains. Share stories, laughter, and quiet moments beneath the Himalayan dusk.

Chapter 05

Work Where Others Vacation

Elevate your remote work experience. High-speed connectivity meets unparalleled mountain views, ensuring your productivity is as inspired as your surroundings.

Chapter 06

Explore Jibhi

Step outside and let the mountains guide you. From hidden trails to panoramic vistas, every path is an invitation to discover the untamed beauty of the Himalayas.

Chapter 07

End The Day Under A Sky Full Of Stars

As darkness falls, the sky transforms into a brilliant canvas. Find peace in the vastness of the universe from the comfort of our serene retreat.

Some Places Are Visited.
Others Become Part Of You.
