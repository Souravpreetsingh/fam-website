# Flamingo aur Maina (FAM) — Luxury Hospitality

## North Star: "Cinematic Serenity"
The brand identity is rooted in the intersection of nature (Flamingo) and storytelling (Maina). It feels like a modern editorial magazine for luxury travel—minimal, handcrafted, and emotionally resonant.

## Colors
- **Primary:** `#234B32` (Deep Forest Green - Depth and heritage)
- **Secondary:** `#F8F4EC` (Warm Cream - Softness and organic warmth)
- **Accent:** `#C9A86A` (Golden Sand - Luxury and refinement)
- **Dark:** `#1B1B1B` (Rich Charcoal - Strong contrast and legibility)
- **White:** `#FFFFFF` (Pure White - Cleanliness and space)
- **Muted:** `#6B7280` (Medium Gray - Secondary information)

## Typography
- **Headings:** `Playfair Display` (Serif - Elegant, timeless, and authoritative)
- **Body:** `Inter` (Sans-serif - Modern, readable, and balanced)
- **Hero:** Extra large, high-contrast serif typography with generous letter-spacing.

## Component Language
- **Roundness:** Large, organic curves (24px+) for containers and images.
- **Buttons:** Fully rounded (pill) with subtle scaling and magnetic hover states.
- **Spacing:** Oversized vertical margins (120px+) to create luxury "breathing room."
- **Grid:** 12-column responsive grid with a max-width of 1400px.
- **Imagery:** Large editorial crops with soft shadows and zoom-on-hover effects.
- **Shadows:** Very soft, diffused shadows to maintain a flat yet layered look.
- **Glassmorphism:** Subtle blur effects for sticky navigation and interactive overlays.

## Interaction
- Reveal-on-scroll animations for all sections.
- Magnetic hover effects on primary CTAs.
- Smooth scrolling and parallax-ready container structures.
