Destinations

Places That Tell Stories

Each destination around FAM has a character, a rhythm, and a reason to visit. Here are the places our guests love most.

01
🌊 Closest to FAM

Jibhi Waterfall

Cascading waters surrounded by lush forest. The closest nature escape from FAM—a short, scenic walk through village paths. The sound of rushing water greets you long before you see the falls.

📍 500 m 🚶 5 min walk ✅ Easy 🌦 All seasons
02
🏔 Alpine Lake Trek

Serolsar Lake

A beautiful 2 km trek from Jalori Pass through enchanting forests of oak and rhododendron, leading to a crystal-clear alpine mirror lake. The trail is as rewarding as the destination.

📍 17 km + 2 km trek 🚶 ~1.5 hr total ✅ Moderate 🌦 May–Oct
03
🌴 High-Altitude Pass

Jalori Pass

Stunning high-altitude views at 3,120 metres. One of Himachal's most scenic mountain passes, with sweeping panoramas of the Himalayas that stretch endlessly in every direction.

📍 15 km 🚗 ~45 min drive ✅ Moderate 🌦 Mar–Jun, Sep–Nov
04
🏛 Heritage Tower

Chehni Kothi

An ancient tower with historic architecture, standing tall in a heritage village dating back centuries. A step back in time through local village paths and traditional culture.

📍 8 km 🚗 ~25 min drive ✅ Easy 🌦 All seasons
05
🏛 Historic Fort

Chaini Fort

A historic fort perched on a ridge offering panoramic views of the surrounding valleys. The trek to the top is an adventure through oak forests and ancient stone pathways.

📍 12 km 🚗 ~35 min drive ✅ Moderate 🌦 All seasons
06
🌸 Riverside Paradise

Mini Thailand

A hidden riverside paradise in the heart of Jibhi. Crystal-blue waters surrounded by boulders and pine trees—perfect for a peaceful afternoon dip and lazy picnics by the river.

📍 2 km 🚗 10 min drive ✅ Easy 🌦 Mar–Jun
07
🌄 Meadow Fort Trek

Raghupur Fort Trek

A high-altitude meadow trek leading to an ancient fort with sweeping views of the Dhauladhar range. Wildflowers, alpine pastures, and a sense of standing on top of the world.

📍 22 km 🚗 ~1 hr drive ✅ Challenging 🌦 May–Oct
08
⛫ Ancient Temple

Shringa Rishi Temple

A centuries-old riverside temple dedicated to Sage Shringa. The peaceful atmosphere, sound of the flowing river, and intricate woodwork make it a serene cultural stop.

📍 5 km 🚗 ~15 min drive ✅ Easy 🌦 All seasons
09
🌱 Valley Escape

Tirthan Valley

Trout fishing, riverside walks, and pristine natural beauty along the Tirthan River. A serene escape into one of Himachal's most picturesque valleys, perfect for slow travellers.

📍 20 km 🚗 ~30 min drive ✅ Easy 🌦 Mar–Jun, Sep–Nov
10
🏛 UNESCO World Heritage

Great Himalayan National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage Site home to exotic wildlife, alpine meadows, and breathtaking Himalayan vistas. A must-visit for nature lovers, birdwatchers, and serious trekkers.

📍 35 km 🚗 ~1 hr drive ✅ Mod–Challenging 🌦 Apr–Jun, Sep–Oct
